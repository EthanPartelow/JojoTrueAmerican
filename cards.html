Game Rules JOJO Stand Powers
1. The Fool (0) –
Ignores penalties from Stands.
2. The Magician (1) –
You can lose a challenge once and move past it without winning. If you win all challenges without using this power, move 3 spaces.
3. The High Priestess (2) –
When you win the Hamaon challenge, pour your beer into the solo cup and the next player has to finish it before beginning their next challenge. That beer still counts as one of your drinks.
4. The Empress (3) –
When you lose the Hamaon challenge, give out one shot to 3 different players and move forward 1 space.
5. The Emperor (4) –
Make one other player chug their drink if that player is within 2 spaces of your partner. (2 Uses)
6. The Hierophant (5) –
Make someone close their eyes during a task. (3 Uses)
7. The Lovers (6) –
If somebody uses their power on you, they are also affected.
8. The Chariot (7) –
Move double the spaces (3 times), but drink twice as much in those zones. Extra drinks do not apply twice to the same zone even if you use Chariot twice in that zone.
9. Strength (8) -
If you are able to chug your beer within the first two spaces of a zone, you can move two extra spaces forward from the space you chugged the beer at. (2 Uses)
10. The Hermit (9) –
All other players tell this player their stand power at the start of the game. After moving through 2 zones, take a new stand power.
11. Wheel of Fortune (10) –
Flip a coin each time you would move, if heads move 2 spaces, if tails move 0. If you flip tails, move 2 spaces in the next turn.
12. Justice (11) –
You can share task victories with your partner. (2 Uses)
13. The Hanged Man (12) –
You can turn off the lights for a full round. (2 Uses)
14. Death (13) –
Make somebody reshuffle their card into the deck and draw a new one during their stand power. (2 Uses) It stops the stand power.
15. Temperance (14) –
Lava does not affect you, but you must still follow the game rules to move forward.
16. The Devil (15) –
Each time you are penalized, choose another player to perform the penalty as well.
17. The Tower (16) –
You can declare that a player can’t use their power for the rest of the zone they are in. (2 Uses)
18. The Star (17) –
Move forward 3 spaces. (1 Use) Unaffected by The World.
19. The Moon (18) –
(Option 1)(1 Use) Make an opponent chug their drink, which must be at least half full. (Option 2)(1 use) Can move through one zone without finishing beer. Both options apply to penalty drinks. Can not select both options.
20. The Sun (19) –
Before starting the game, place 1 new task on 1 space of their choice that must be completed before the player can move on. If no other challenges are in play, then this power allows for 2 uses. (1 Use)
21. Judgment (20) –
Interrupt someone’s categories turn and choose for them. (2 Uses)
22. The World (21) –
Move someone back a zone, drink again for that zone. (1 Use)
